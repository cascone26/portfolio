# Blog Post Outline: Website Lead Generation for Small Business

## Title
"How to Turn Your Small Business Website Into a Lead Generation Machine (7 Proven Strategies)"

## Meta Description

## Article Structure

### 1. Introduction: Your Website Should Work While You Sleep
**Key Points:**
- Hook: Most small business websites are digital business cards, not lead generators
- Problem: Website gets traffic but no phone calls, emails, or customers
- Promise: 7 specific strategies that turn visitors into leads
- Real example: Local contractor who went from 2 leads/month to 25 leads/month

### 2. Strategy #1: Optimize Your Contact Forms (The Low-Hanging Fruit)
**Key Points:**
- Why most contact forms fail (too many fields, poor placement)
- The 3-field rule: Name, email, phone OR message
- Smart form placement: above the fold + end of key pages
- Mobile form optimization (tap targets, autocomplete)
- Example: Before/after conversion rates

### 3. Strategy #2: Create Irresistible Lead Magnets
**Key Points:**
- What is a lead magnet (free resource for contact info)
- Best lead magnets for different business types:
  - Service businesses: Free consultation, quote, checklist
  - Retail: Discount codes, buying guides
  - B2B: Templates, worksheets, mini-courses
- Where to place lead magnets (popup timing, exit-intent)
- Example: HVAC company's "Winter Preparation Checklist" case study

### 4. Strategy #3: Master Your Call-to-Action (CTA) Game
**Key Points:**
- The anatomy of a high-converting CTA
- Action words that work: "Get", "Start", "Claim", "Download"
- Button colors and placement (contrast, whitespace)
- Multiple CTAs per page without being pushy
- A/B testing your CTAs (simple tools)

### 5. Strategy #4: Build Trust With Social Proof
**Key Points:**
- Why trust matters more than features for lead generation
- Types of social proof: reviews, testimonials, case studies, logos
- Where to place social proof on your site
- Getting more reviews (the ethical way)
- Real numbers: How social proof impacts conversion rates

### 6. Strategy #5: Speed Up Your Site (Slow = No Leads)
**Key Points:**
- Page speed's direct impact on lead generation
- Quick wins: image compression, caching, hosting
- Mobile speed matters most (60%+ of traffic)
- Testing tools: Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix
- Case study: Restaurant that gained 40% more reservations

### 7. Strategy #6: Optimize for Local Search Intent
**Key Points:**
- Local intent keywords ("plumber near me", "best pizza [city]")
- Google Business Profile optimization for lead generation
- Location pages for multi-location businesses
- Local schema markup basics
- Reviews and local SEO connection

### 8. Strategy #7: Follow Up Fast (Speed to Lead Matters)
**Key Points:**
- The 5-minute rule: Why speed matters for conversion
- Automated responses vs personal follow-up
- Email sequences for nurturing leads
- CRM basics for small businesses (simple options)
- Script examples for first contact calls

### 9. Putting It All Together: Your 30-Day Action Plan
**Key Points:**
- Week 1: Audit current forms and CTAs
- Week 2: Create one lead magnet, optimize speed
- Week 3: Add social proof, improve trust signals
- Week 4: Set up tracking and follow-up systems
- What to measure: conversion rate, lead quality, cost per lead

### 10. Conclusion: From Website to Lead Generation Machine
**Key Points:**
- Recap of 7 strategies
- Realistic expectations (improvements take time)
- When to get professional help vs DIY
- Call-to-action: Free website audit offer
