## Title
"Learning Management System for Small Schools: 8 Essential Features Your Website Needs to Compete with Big Education Platforms"

## Meta Description
"Build a powerful learning management system into your school website. Essential LMS features that help small private schools compete with expensive platforms like Blackboard and Canvas."

## Outline Structure

### 1. **Introduction: Why Small Schools Need Their Own LMS**
- Small private schools can't afford $15,000/year for Canvas or Blackboard
- Parents expect digital learning tools in 2026
- Custom LMS features cost less than third-party subscriptions
- Schools that build their own systems keep 100% control over data and features

### 2. **Assignment Distribution and Collection System**
- Teachers upload assignments directly through admin panel
- Students download materials from secure student portal
- File upload system for homework submission
- Automatic timestamps and late submission tracking
- Grade book integration

### 3. **Parent Communication Portal**
- Real-time progress updates for parents
- Direct messaging between teachers and families
- Automated alerts for missing assignments or attendance issues
- Calendar integration for important dates and events
- Mobile-responsive design for busy parents

### 4. **Student Progress Tracking and Analytics**
- Individual student dashboards showing current grades
- Progress visualization charts parents can understand
- Attendance tracking with automated notifications
- Behavioral notes and achievement tracking
- Report card generation

### 5. **Resource Library and Digital Materials**
- Secure file hosting for textbooks, worksheets, videos
- Subject-organized content that teachers can update easily
- Search functionality for students to find specific materials
- Version control for curriculum updates
- Integration with Google Drive or Dropbox

### 6. **Calendar and Scheduling Integration**
- School-wide calendar with events and deadlines
- Teacher-specific calendars for each class
- Parent view of their child's schedule and upcoming assignments
- Integration with popular calendar apps (Google, Apple, Outlook)
- Automated reminders for important dates

### 7. **Discussion Forums and Collaboration Tools**
- Class-specific discussion boards for projects
- Moderated forums where students can ask questions
- Peer collaboration spaces for group assignments
- Teacher oversight and participation controls
- Safe, supervised environment for student interaction

### 8. **Simple Grade Book with Parent Access**
- Easy grade entry for teachers
- Weighted categories for different assignment types
- Parent access to see grades in real-time
- Progress tracking over time
- Customizable grading scales for different subjects

### 9. **Mobile-First Design Considerations**
- Responsive design that works on all devices
- Parent app or mobile-optimized web interface
- Push notifications for important updates
- Offline access to downloaded materials
- Fast loading times on slower connections
