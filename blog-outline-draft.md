## Title
"How to Build a Website for Your Dance Studio (Complete Guide for Dance Entrepreneurs)"

## Meta Description
"Transform your dance studio from hobby business to professional academy. Learn essential website features that streamline registration, payments, and showcase your programs to attract more students."

---

## Section 1: Why Your Dance Studio Needs More Than Social Media
**Key Points:**
- Facebook pages look unprofessional to parents researching studios
- Instagram doesn't handle class registration or payments
- Professional websites establish credibility and trust
- Parents expect online registration and class information
- Competition analysis - successful studios have professional websites
- SEO benefits for local discovery

## Section 2: Essential Features That Convert Visitors Into Students
**Key Points:**
- Online class registration with capacity limits
- Integrated payment processing for classes and recital fees
- Detailed class descriptions with age groups and skill levels
- Teacher bio pages with credentials and photos
- Student showcase/gallery (with proper permissions)
- Testimonials from satisfied parents

## Section 3: Streamlining Operations With the Right Tools
**Key Points:**
- Automated waitlist management for popular classes
- Parent portal for account management and payments
- Digital forms for waivers and medical information
- Attendance tracking integration
- Costume/recital fee management
- Communication tools for studio announcements

## Section 4: Showcasing Your Programs and Building Trust
**Key Points:**
- Professional photography of studio spaces
- Video previews of classes and performances
- Clear pricing structure and package options
- Recital and competition achievements
- Safety protocols and studio policies
- Professional certifications and accreditations

## Section 5: Local SEO That Gets You Found by Nearby Families
**Key Points:**
- Google My Business optimization
- Local keyword targeting (city + "dance studio")
- Location-based landing pages if multiple studios
- Parent review management strategy
- Community event promotion
- Local dance competition and recital promotion

## Section 6: Avoiding Common Dance Studio Website Mistakes
**Key Points:**
- Outdated class schedules and pricing
- Poor mobile experience for busy parents
- No clear call-to-action for trial classes
- Overwhelming navigation with too many options
- Missing contact information and studio location
- Slow loading times that frustrate users

## Section 7: Getting Started - Your Action Plan
**Key Points:**
- Content audit checklist for launch
- Photography and video planning
- Registration system setup priorities
- Marketing integration with website launch
- Measuring success with analytics
- Timeline for professional website development
