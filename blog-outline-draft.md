## Article Structure (800 words target):

### 1. Introduction: The Tutoring Business Boom (150 words)
**Key Points:**
- Online tutoring market growth (projected $325B by 2026)
- Post-pandemic shift to digital learning
- Low startup costs vs traditional businesses
- Perfect side hustle for teachers and subject experts
- Why 2026 is the ideal time to start

### 2. Step 1: Define Your Tutoring Niche (120 words)
**Key Points:**
- Subject specialization vs general academics
- Grade level focus (K-12, college, adult learners)
- Test prep opportunities (SAT, ACT, GRE, professional certifications)
- Skills-based tutoring (study skills, executive function)
- Language learning and ESL markets
- Choosing based on expertise + market demand

### 3. Step 2: Business Planning and Legal Setup (100 words)
**Key Points:**
- Business structure (LLC vs sole proprietorship)
- Required licenses and certifications by state
- Insurance considerations for educators
- Tax implications and quarterly payments
- Setting up business banking
- Basic bookkeeping systems

### 4. Step 3: Create Your Online Presence (150 words)
**Key Points:**
- Why you need a professional website (not just marketplace profiles)
- Essential website features for tutors
- Online scheduling and payment systems
- Creating compelling tutor profiles
- Professional headshots and bio writing
- SEO basics for local tutoring searches
- Building credibility with testimonials

### 5. Step 4: Pricing Strategy and Packages (100 words)
**Key Points:**
- Competitive rate research by subject/location
- Hourly vs package pricing models
- Premium pricing for specialized subjects
- Group tutoring opportunities
- Seasonal pricing adjustments
- Payment policies and late cancellation fees

### 6. Step 5: Marketing Your Tutoring Business (120 words)
**Key Points:**
- Local SEO and Google My Business optimization
- Content marketing through educational blog posts
- Social media strategy for parents and students
- Referral programs and incentives
- Partnership opportunities with schools
- Online tutoring platforms vs independent marketing
- Email marketing for retention

### 7. Step 6: Delivering Exceptional Service (60 words)
**Key Points:**
- Setting up your home office/virtual classroom
- Technology requirements and backup plans
- Student assessment and progress tracking
- Communication with parents
- Continuing education and skill development
