## Section 1: Why Piano Teachers Need Professional Websites
**Key Points:**
- Facebook pages and business cards aren't enough in 2026
- Parents research piano teachers online before making contact
- Professional websites establish credibility vs. informal instructors
- Online presence differentiates from conservatory competition
- Impact on student retention and referral rates

## Section 2: Essential Features for Piano Teacher Websites
**Key Points:**
- Online lesson booking and scheduling system
- Student practice tracking and assignment portals
- Recital calendar and event management
- Payment processing for lesson fees and materials
- Teacher credentials and performance background showcase
- Video lesson capabilities and resources library

## Section 3: Showcasing Your Musical Expertise Online
**Key Points:**
- Performance video gallery and audio samples
- Student success stories and recital recordings
- Teaching philosophy and pedagogical approach
- Educational credentials and performance experience
- Specializations (classical, jazz, contemporary, exam prep)
- Age group expertise and curriculum offerings

## Section 4: Student Acquisition Through Your Website
**Key Points:**
- Local SEO for "[city] piano teacher" searches
- Lead capture forms for lesson inquiries
- Free resource downloads (practice sheets, music theory guides)
- Trial lesson booking and consultation scheduling
- Parent testimonials and student progression showcases
- Referral program integration and tracking

## Section 5: Streamlining Lesson Management and Communication
**Key Points:**
- Automated lesson reminders and cancellation policies
- Assignment tracking and practice log access
- Progress reporting for parents and students
- Music library and sheet music distribution
- Makeup lesson scheduling and studio policies
- Payment tracking and invoice generation

## Section 6: Building Long-term Student Relationships
**Key Points:**
- Recital planning and performance opportunities
- Student achievement tracking and celebration
- Parent communication and progress updates
- Advanced student pathways and competition prep
- Studio community building and peer connections
- Alumni network and continued engagement

## Section 7: Technical Setup and Maintenance for Music Educators
**Key Points:**
- Domain name selection for music teaching business
- Audio/video optimization for performance samples
- Mobile-friendly design for busy parents
- Booking system integration with teaching schedule
- Security for student information and payment processing
- Backup systems for lesson materials and student records
